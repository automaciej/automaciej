_Importance vs Difficulty: Do Now, Schedule, Postpone_

**Task Compass** is an iOS app that transforms the overwhelming question "What
should I work on?" into a systematic decision-making process. Using the proven
Eisenhower Matrix methodology, it helps you focus on high-impact work by
combining importance and difficulty rankings to automatically categorize your
tasks into four strategic quadrants: Do First, Schedule, Consider, or Eliminate.

The app leverages a key insight: humans excel at relative comparisons but
struggle with absolute judgments. Instead of asking "How important is this task
on a scale of 1-10?", Task Compass asks "Is Task A more important than Task B?"
You drag tasks to rank them by importance, then by difficulty. This comparative
approach eliminates the "everything feels urgent" trap.

Task Compass integrates seamlessly with Apple Reminders—no data migration
required. Your existing reminders sync automatically while preserving all
context like notes, due dates, and calendar assignments. The result is a set of
automatically organized lists: Do Now, Schedule, and Postpone.

This shows exactly where to focus your time and energy, replacing guesswork with
strategic clarity.
