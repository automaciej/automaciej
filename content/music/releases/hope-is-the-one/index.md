Inspired by the Czech proverb "Naděje umírá poslední" (hope dies last), this single production functions as a heartfelt praise to hope. The song demonstrates a maturation into a rich, ensemble-driven aesthetic that utilizes the full range of Zoe Hayter's theatrical background.

The track features a dynamic build from quiet vulnerability into an energetic, band-driven anthem. The violin work by Rafał Szydłowski acts as the metaphorical "voice of hope," while Maciej Bliziński’s piano and electric bass provide a modern, grounded foundation for Zoe's rustic and angelic vocals.
