When crowds overran Dublin beaches on the hottest day in Ireland since 2022,
Bliziński and his partner climbed down a cliff face to a small, unmapped cove. A
water bottle that slipped on the way down, scratched and dented but holding
water. It became the central metaphor: that the cost of going somewhere worth
going is inseparable from the going. Written in a single sitting that afternoon;
produced in Dolby Atmos.
